--- layout: post title: "(Trying to) be a better person" date: '2017-12-22T15:13:00.002-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2017-12-22T15:13:46.516-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-6733157271478556717 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/12/trying-to-be-better-person.html --- The goal of trying to be a "better" person is unhelpful vague, painful unoriginal, and frankly only becoming of someone with neither intelligence nor ambition. And so, it is perfectly suited to being my goal.

I'm 50% joking. It is an alarmingly obvious goal to have, but I am an alarmingly dumb person so I think it will be useful to have it in the forefront of my mind. As you can probably tell, it is a goal I am more resigned to than excited about. I am splitting my pursuit of "betterness" into 5 areas: mental health, physical health, work, hobbies and social life. Updates in the future, maybe.
