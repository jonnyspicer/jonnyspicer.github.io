--- layout: post title: Balancing the coin date: '2017-10-21T06:57:00.001-07:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2017-10-21T06:57:13.294-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-5639390764685365474 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/10/balancing-coin.html --- Forming good habits is hard, and I struggle with it all the time. I once heard the analogy that forming a good habit is like trying to balance a coin on it's side - it will fall over the first few times but eventually it will stay. When trying to form a good habit, I mustn't get discouraged when I fail, and instead simply keep trying until I succeed.
