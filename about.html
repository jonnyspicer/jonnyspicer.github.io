About Me

I was born and raised on the beautiful island of Guernsey, and have lived here the majority of my life. My career path thus far has had some thrills and spills, the most exciting of which was playing poker full time in 2017. Since then I've finally managed to channel the endless hours I've spent at a computer during my lifetime into something positive, and I'm now a C# developer for Cortex.

I play chess, enjoy climbing and running, and I'm trying really hard to be one of those people that reads a lot of books so I can seem smart when I go on dates. Fortunately, I never go on any dates.
