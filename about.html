About

I was born and raised on the beautiful island of Guernsey, and have lived here the majority of my life. My career has taken some winding and unexpected turns; from studying law at university, to sports trading, a slightly more logical transition into full time poker, before returning to the real world by becoming a software developer.

I play chess, enjoy the gym and running, and I love esports so wholeheartedly that it defines my sleep schedule far more than the lousy sun ever could.

The goal of this website is to be a public journal of sorts, and as such was inspired almost entirely by this lyric from Childish Gambino's 2011 debut album, 'Camp'. With all that in mind, I endeavour to write as honestly I can, often diarising my own mental health struggles, as well as writing about technology, media, social issues, diet and fitness. I write a post daily and I love doing so, it's been both cathartic and rewarding, and I hope to continue doing it for years to come.

I especially love talking to people about the things I've written there, so if you have anything you'd like to say then please shoot me an email at spicerjonny@gmail.com.

Enjoy!
