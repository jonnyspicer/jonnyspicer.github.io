--- layout: mendokusai title: Taking things slow date: '2018-01-07T09:21:00.000-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-01-07T09:21:00.763-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-767749679157391187 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/01/taking-things-slow.html --- When trying to make positive changes in my life, I often want to do everything at once, to wake up tomorrow and make a whole bunch of drastic lifestyle changes and somehow just try really hard to make them stick. But that is obviously dumb, even though I try it every time. So this week, my only goal is to try to eat and sleep well, and then next week if that goes well I can work on something else.
