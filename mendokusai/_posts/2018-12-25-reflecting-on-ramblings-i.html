I think Ramblings I is probably my favourite post from Mendokusai. Written a year ago tomorrow, it was when I was in the eye of the storm, a surprisingly moment of tranquility amid a torrent of anguish.

The sentiment I expressed is one I would do well to remember more often, if I were ever to do some kind of daily affirmation type-thing, then this would likely be a part of it. 'Why do I have to be so ground down in order to feel so much love for the people around me?'
