--- layout: mendokusai title: Productive vacation date: '2017-12-19T07:10:00.001-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2017-12-19T07:10:52.117-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-2224389515047740583 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/12/productive-vacation.html --- I've decided to take a little bit (more) of time off from playing poker, but I don't want to have a complete holiday, I think I would get bored of the beach pretty quickly. So I have been thinking about what I can do with my time and have decided to try and do several 24 hour "challenges" in different things, to see how much I can improve at a skill in just one day. So far my ideas are thus:


  • Write a short story 
  • Write a stand up routine or comedy sketch
  • Start a new speed game from scratch and submit to the speedrun.com leaderboards by the end of the day
  • Get my blitz chess rating up as much as possible, follow a study plan
  • Improve my Spanish in as much as possible, with some kind of tangible goal at the end, either to write a poem in Spanish, translate my short story into Spanish, have some kind of spoken conversation etc.

Good luck me.
