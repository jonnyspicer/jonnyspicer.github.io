Once again far, far too exhausted to write anything lengthy or thought out tonight. So, just an observation of the day - hope is severely underrated. I would say it is practically vital in order to have a fulfilling human experience. We are, by nature, never satisfied with the present, and if we don't have hope for a better future, the situations starts to become bleak very fast.

Sure, I should just focus on getting better at appreciating what I already have, and being satisfied with the way things are. But that would go against years of conditioning myself to think otherwise - so I am going to settle for trying to incubate as much hope for positive future change as possible.
