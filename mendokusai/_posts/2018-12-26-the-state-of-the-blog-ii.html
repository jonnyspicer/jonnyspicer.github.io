A couple of weeks ago, I wrote about whether or not I still wanted this blog to be purely 'for me' or if I wanted to try to share more. Today, the answer is definitely, definitely that I still want it to be for me.

Even considering trying to market the blog a little made me feel under a lot of pressure to write longer, higher quality posts every day, but the reality is that if I am tired and depressed (which is most days), then I just want to write a short post. And writing something short is fine, because it still serves the purpose I had when I started this blog, which was just to get in the habit of writing something every day. Even if that something is short because I'm having a rough time, I think that's better than nothing, or forcing myself to write something long.

I feel like my posts this last week have been a bit short and maybe not quite as good as some were the week before that. I'm not gonna beat myself about it though, and I am about halfway through my 2018 year in review piece and it's looking like a long one, so you have that to look forward to.
