--- layout: mendokusai title: Variance I date: '2017-11-05T04:46:00.001-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2017-11-05T04:46:16.233-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-5465616975355616978 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/11/variance-i.html --- My best friend in high school used to say to me "hope for the best, prepare for the worst, something in the middle will probably happen". From what I remember he was usually doing about girls, and the worst almost always happened, but at least I was sufficiently prepared for it. Thinking about it now, I think it might be good advice for poker variance too - hope you will run well, study and mentally prepare as if you will lose every flip ever, but chances are you'll probably run at EV.
