--- layout: mendokusai title: Being a role model date: '2018-01-10T14:03:00.004-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-01-10T14:03:54.121-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-6793048906714688317 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/01/being-role-model.html --- Even talking about being a role model feels a little arrogant to me. I do not think anyone should look up to me at all, but sometimes it seems like I don't really have a choice in the matter. I would rather nobody looked up to me, but you can't exactly turn round and tell somebody not to. So, I guess the only thing left is to try really hard at whatever I do, and hopefully encourage people around me to do the same.
