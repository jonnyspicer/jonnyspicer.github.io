Today I am happy to announce I made my first open source contribution! Admittedly all I did was correct a handful of typos in the project docs, but nonetheless it felt good when my pull request got accepted. Even if this was not a contribution of any serious significance or skill, I am hoping that it marks the first of many, and will serve as a stepping stone towards making meaningful additions to bigger projects.

Thank you so much to David Pinheiro for being so kind with me and my first pull request. Check out Mole, a nifty CLI tool that allows easy creation of SSH tunnels.
