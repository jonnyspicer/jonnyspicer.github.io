In my recent quest for sad songs, I have rediscovered Julien Baker, affectionately known to her fans as the Sad Queen. She is definitely worth of that moniker, her music is haunting and sombre, but with a purity and beauty that make it more than worth listening to.

She has a great set on KEXP, 10/10 heartily recommending.
