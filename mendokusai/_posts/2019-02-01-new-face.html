Today I shaved my face down to the skin for the first time in around 4 years. My chin has not seen the light of day for the last 1500 of them - I wonder what its take on the shift in the global political climate is.

I expected to hate it, but I actually kinda don't - although I certainly don't love it either. But, given that everything else is changing, this felt like time for a facial change as well - time for a new face.

I wonder if I'll start getting IDed in bars again now.
