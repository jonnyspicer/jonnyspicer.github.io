--- layout: mendokusai title: Death Grips date: '2018-04-18T13:28:00.004-07:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-04-18T13:28:33.709-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-1210085430941316051 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/04/death-grips.html --- Death Grips seem to be the most talked about musicians on the internet. Divisive, controversial and experimental, I am sort of embarrassed I haven't listened to them sooner, given that I am always looking for art that is unusual or pushing boundaries, rather than necessarily beautiful. This week I finally got round to listening to their breakout album, The Money Store, and it is a banger. Not sure if it is quite life changing, perhaps after a few more listens, but it is certainly damn good.
