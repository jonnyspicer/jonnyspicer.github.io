--- layout: mendokusai title: Running date: '2018-02-04T14:31:00.001-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-02-04T14:31:21.301-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-6086502776105493008 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/02/running.html --- I went for a run today, and I can't remember the last time I did that, it could easily be over a year ago. In retrospect, going with a competitive mid-long distance runner was potentially a mistake, given I pushed myself far too hard. After running 2.5km in 15 mins (which I was actually pleasantly surprised with), I had to stop... and then I had to sit down... and then I had to lie down... and then a couple of people pulled over to ask if I was alright. Fortunately after a few minutes on the ground I was fine, and even ran some of the way back home. It was weirdly fun despite being very uncomfortable for a lot of it.
