--- layout: mendokusai title: Alcohol date: '2017-12-17T18:48:00.001-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2017-12-17T18:48:47.739-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-3591419492567794182 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/12/alcohol.html --- The relationship I have with alcohol is strange. I like drinking a lot, but have always felt guilty about it, because it is unhealthy. However, my understanding is that it is one of the most powerful anxiolytic drugs available, so I think it stands to reason that I would benefit from its effects and enjoy consuming it. If it makes me happier, but is has some negative health effects when consistently consumed in moderate-large quantities, is it a net negative on my life? It is hard to tell.
