--- layout: mendokusai title: Sugar Pine 7 date: '2018-01-04T15:05:00.001-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-01-04T15:05:33.599-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-5505559660982757119 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/01/sugar-pine-7.html --- It's interesting how involved I (and presumably other people) can get involved in shows, or in this case, Youtube shows with weird meta concepts. Sugar Pine 7 is a bizarre pseudo-vlog where it's never obvious what is scripted and what is improvised, or even when the characters are doing a bit or being real. Or even whether or not they are characters or their real selves. It is definitely genius.

In one way, it is just a shitty Youtube show. But the people are involved are smart and inspiring, and watching the show makes me happier than it probably should. Is that a bad thing?
