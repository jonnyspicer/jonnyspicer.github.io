--- layout: mendokusai title: Finishing what has been started date: '2018-01-09T14:39:00.002-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-01-09T14:39:24.172-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-7747306477446235564 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/01/finishing-what-has-been-started.html --- Throughout my life, I have been often failed to finish what I started, and often gave up when things became difficult. I have been thinking a lot this week whether or not I want to continue playing poker or doing something else. This morning I remembered that several times over the past few months I have promised myself I will keep playing poker until I absolutely cannot any longer. So, I'm going to take some more time off to compose myself further, and then back to finishing what I've started.
