--- layout: mendokusai title: What's the point? date: '2018-01-30T13:17:00.002-08:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2018-01-30T13:17:35.263-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-2647158757502249052 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2018/01/whats-point.html --- I often feel guilty about how I choose to spend my time if I am not doing something I think is productive. I feel like I ought to spend every waking moment producing something or trying to better myself or whatever. But this week I thought that the reason to do something "productive" and the reason to do anything else at all is exactly the same. I do them for no other reason than because I can.
