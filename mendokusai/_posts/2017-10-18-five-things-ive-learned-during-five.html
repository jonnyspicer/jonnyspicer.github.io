  1. Leaving my comfort zone is good, but I have to keep doing it. Living a comfortable life is boring and unfulfilling.
  2. Learning a language is a lot easier when you live in the country that speaks it - but it's still difficult and you have to work at it every day.
  3. Anonymity is amazing, I never want to be famous in any way.
  4. Good habits are really hard to form, but offering freerolls is a good way to get started.
  5. Friendships need work, especially when you live thousands of miles away. I have to put the time in to talk to the people I care about.
