Today Gerard Way released a new song titled Baby You're A Haunted House, his first release in two years, with pretty much no warning. It's quite Hallo-meme-y, but I like it a lot.

Like everyone else who experienced teenage angst in the mid to late oughties, I love Gerard's music, it got me through some tough times and went on to shape my now (hopefully) more refined music tastes. As an artist he has clearly grown a lot, from literally screaming out the lyrics, "I'm not OK", to a much more mature sound on his later solo work, and generally seeming at peace, something I think a lot of his fanbase, drawn to his music because he sang about struggles they identified with, should try to emulate. Cheers, Gerard.
