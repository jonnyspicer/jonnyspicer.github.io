--- layout: mendokusai title: 'Awful pseudo-deep post about emotions #4713' date: '2017-10-30T08:31:00.000-07:00' author: Jonny Spicer tags: modified_time: '2017-10-30T08:31:10.449-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-7290984073042596032 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/10/awful-pseudo-deep-post-about-emotions.html --- My emotions feel disproportionately strong compared to how long they last. One day I feel intensely unhappy, and the next carefree and joyous, with no significant events in between. Our emotions are the products of our situation, but seem so precarious, nothing more than a good night's sleep will drastically change them. What is the point of these emotions then? I need to get better at noticing them without reacting to them.
